about
the studio

Paper + Pencil is a minimal design practice focused on tactile, monochromatic storytelling. Founded in 2021, we believe that constraints (black & white, paper, ink) breed creativity. Every project starts with a sketch on paper — no screens allowed until the idea is solid.

exhibitions: Frankfurt book fair 2024 Design Biennale 2023

— Clients include small presses, cultural foundations, and independent authors who appreciate the grain of paper.
